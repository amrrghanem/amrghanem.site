About Me

I am Dr. Amr Ghanem, an advanced structural designer and researcher specializing in multi-hazard resilience assessments, seismic analysis, and AI-driven structural health monitoring. I have a Ph.D. in Civil Engineering with a focus on structural engineering and have worked on innovative projects addressing the resilience of infrastructures under extreme conditions.

Research Interests

  • Multi-hazard assessment and risk mitigation
  • Structural performance under extreme loading
  • Smart sensors for structural health monitoring
  • Innovative materials in construction
  • Building Information Modeling (BIM) integration
  • AI and deep learning techniques in structural analysis

Highlighted Projects

Seismic Fragility Analysis of Bridges

Developed fragility models for skewed bridges under seismic conditions.

AI-based Structural Health Monitoring

Created AI-powered tools for detecting structural damage using smart sensors and real-time data collection.
